About Us

Razumovsky Construction LLC has been building quality homes in Portland, Oregon, since 2008. Founded by Aleksey "Aleks" Razumovsky, the company specializes in foundations, flooring, and roofing, delivering expert craftsmanship on time and within budget. As a licensed Master Craftsman and Oregon State Inspector, Aleks is committed to building homes with lasting quality, trust, and excellence
